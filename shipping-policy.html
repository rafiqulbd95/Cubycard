Returning Policy

How will my order be shipped to me?

Small orders ‘1 to 5 cards’ will be send by regular post. Larger order will be send by packaging with tracking number.

How long will it take to ship my order?

We will process your order within 2-5 business days, depending on whether you choose a custom or standard design. Once your order ships, it may take up to 1 - 2 weeks for your order to arrive, depending on your country. Most orders arrive within 1 weeks.
