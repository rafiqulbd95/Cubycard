About Us

The future of networking

Our mission is to take networking to a new level. By replacing the traditional business card with the digital business card, we are able to add additional useful elements to the network experience. The development of the app is an ongoing process. We are always looking for new ways to fine tune Cubycard and make our service as efficiently as possible. At Cubycard we help, both large and small companies, through a fun and easy user-experience, expand their professional network.

Who we are

The driven force of Cubycard consists of a small team with four people, each with its own specialty. We firmly believe in the futureproof transition Cubycard makes compared to outdated business cards.

Our vision lies at the foundation of our diligent cooperation, meaning we are passionate about achieving the corporate goal of Cubycard within a joint work process. If you are interested in joining our team, send a message to info@cubycard.com.

Sustainability

Our mission to develop a futureproof networking system reaches way beyond our belief in the digital business card. Paper made business cards contribute highly to the single-use littering problem. We want to be a part of a sustainable shift within the networking business. One tangible Cubycard gives you the possibility to make limitless connections, without using one piece of paper. Say goodbye to single-use business cards. Save trees and contribute to the environment.
