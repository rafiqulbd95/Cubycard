General

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, we do.

How much does shipping cost?

We offer FREE worldwide shipping.

How long will my order take to arrive?

We have a 5-day processing time, due to the fact that we need to program and print the Cubycards. How long your order takes to arrive depends on your shipping destination. Shipping within Europe will take approximately one week. Destinations out of the EU will receive their Cubycard within two weeks of time.

Do I need to pay a monthly fee?

No, you only need to purchase the tangible Cubycard with a single payment, no strings attached. The app is free of charge and always will be. However, we might introduce a premium account in the future with more features.

Does my connection need the app to receive my contact details?

No, that’s one of the absolute advantages of Cubycard. The person who you would like to connect with doesn’t need to install the Cubycard app to receive your contact details.

Do I need a physical Cubycard to use the app?

No, you can start using the app without the physical Cubycard. However, you will miss out on additional advantages the card has to offer such as the easy user experience and a quicker way of transferring contact details. It’s up to you if you choose to purchase a card or not.
